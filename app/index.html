Who am I?

I'm a Swedish front-end web developer currently looking for a Job. My main areas of expertise are JavaScript, PHP and WordPress.

I studied web development and graphic design for 2 years in Sweden and I'm now studying web development for one semester in Canberra, Australia.

I also got the opportunity to be an intern at NY Collective, for 3 month. There I got to learn how it all really works and I was instantly hooked.

Most of what I know hasn't come from school or as an intern, but "self studying". I don't like calling it self studying because that makes it sound boring. I read articles/books, watch youtube-videos, do tutorials etc. about web development as a hobby.
